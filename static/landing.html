About the centre

A dedicated space for donors, built for comfort and care

Shri Satya Sai Blood Centre in Cidco runs around the clock, with air-conditioned donation rooms and proper collection and storage facilities on site. Whether you're a first-time donor or here to help a patient in need, our staff is on hand day and night.

We're rated 4.5 out of 5 by donors who've visited — many mention the comfortable setup and genuinely caring staff as reasons they keep coming back.
